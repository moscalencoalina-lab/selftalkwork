Perceptual map analysis

A perceptual map is a visual tool that displays how target customers perceive competing brands across two dimensions that matter most to their buying decision. It reveals white spaces no competitor currently occupies, crowded segments with established demand, and where a brand stands relative to the competition — translating customer perception data into a single, decision-ready picture. The scores below are based on verified competitive research; circle sizes reflect market presence and funding stage.
